Full Job Description

At Komatsu Australia you'll be part of an inclusive culture where all our people thrive, reflected in our purpose: creating value together. We encourage people with diverse backgrounds to apply for this role.

The Opportunity:

As a Boilermaker with Komatsu, you will carry out maintenance and repairs on Komatsu mobile equipment at the iconic superpit. Working on our customer site, your role will be delivering excellent customer service and high quality maintenance solutions. With the potential to learn and develop your skills, Komatsu has extensive training and opportunities available.

Job Details:

  • Annualised Earnings: $181,370 - $187,931 per year + Super + Regional Allowance + Bonus
  • Roster: 7 days, 4 off, 7 nights, 3 off
  • Role Located: Residential Kalgoorlie role. Relocation allowance available.

Komatsu can provide you with:

  • Families Policy: 16 weeks of paid primary carer parental leave, 4 weeks of secondary carer leave, plus many more family friendly benefits
  • Financial: Income Protection Insurance provided through our company superannuation fund
  • Career: Recognition, awards, flexibility, further education studies and internal career development
  • Health: Corporate discount with Bupa, Total Wellbeing Solution with Telus Health
  • Travel: Discounts with Avis, Qantas, Crown, IHG and travel agents
  • Referral Program: $3,000 referral payment for referring a successful new employee to Komatsu

The Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:

  • Cert III Heavy Fabrication Certificate or equivalent required
  • Experience repairing heavy mobile equipment
  • Must have a commitment to safety for yourself and others
  • Good understanding of computer systems and Microsoft Office

Komatsu offers a zero-harm culture in the workplace and competitive remuneration package.
